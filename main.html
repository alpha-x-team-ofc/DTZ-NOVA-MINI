How to Connect:

  1. Enter your WhatsApp number with country code
  2. Click "Generate Pair Code"
  3. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  4. Go to Settings → Linked Devices
  5. Tap "Link a Device"
  6. Choose "Link with QR code" or "Link with phone number"
  7. Scan the QR code or enter the pairing code
  8. Wait for connection confirmation (2-3 minutes)
  9. Type .menu to see all commands

Note: Keep your phone connected to internet during pairing process.
